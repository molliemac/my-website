About Me

Hi, my name's Mollie and I am a web developer with a passion for technology, specifically as it relates to marketing. Your website is the "hub" of your marketing efforts and having a strong online presence for potential customers to gather information and capture those leads is crucial to the growth of your business. Having a beautiful, intuitive website is great but without the marketing strategy and technology properly integrated into your site, it is impossible to gather the analytics needed on your web visitors to make smart business decisions. Building out websites and integrating with your sales and marketing platforms is where I step in. Have a website but need help selecting and/or integrating marketing tech for your business? Great, let's work together! Already have a (semi or complete) marketing strategy in place but need a web presence that better demonstrates your brand and helps further nurture leads down your sales funnel? Awesome, let's chat!
